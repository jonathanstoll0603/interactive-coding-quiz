Coding Assessment

When you hit the "start quiz" key, you will be prompted with 4 different questions in sequential order. You will have exactly 60 seconds to answer all of the questions. If you get the answer right, a prompt will appear stating that you got the right answer! If you choose wrong, the correct answer will appear in green and a prompt will appear stating that you got the wrong answer. You can also access past highscores to track your progress. Good luck and have fun!
